Sense Your city

As a child I suffered from asthma. Any person who has gone through similar experience becomes acutely aware of the air we are breathing. As I was looking for data related hackathons I came across this awesome dataset by DataCanvas. Dataset contains measurements such as temperature, dust level, collected by citizen diy sensors. Sense your city is a pretty exciting project put together by Data Canvas. I live in bay area now, so I got curious and started exploring the data from sensors located in San Francisco. Code is in github.
